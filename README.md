You know the feeling. You opened a tab to do one thing, and somehow half an hour
was gone. You didn't decide to spend it. It just happened, and you surface feeling
a little emptier than before.

I got tired of losing hours I never chose to spend. So I built this.

Now when I open Instagram out of habit, it doesn't open. I get a slow breath, a
quiet reminder that I'm alive right now, and a bit of nature sound. The pull just
lets go, and a few seconds later I remember what I actually sat down to do.

<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/redirect.gif" alt="Typing instagram.com in the address bar and landing on the calm page instead" width="760">
</p>

It's yours to shape. Block whatever steals your time. Instagram is blocked out of
the box, but add X, Reddit, YouTube, whatever your thing is, right from the calm
page. No menus to dig through.

<p align="center">
  <img src="assets/block-list.gif" alt="Adding and removing a site from the block list, right on the calm page" width="760">
</p>

No accounts. No tracking. No feed. Just you, a breath, and the thing you meant to
do instead.

## Get it

There's no build step. Open `chrome://extensions` (or `edge://extensions`), turn
on **Developer mode**, click **Load unpacked**, and pick this folder. That's it.
